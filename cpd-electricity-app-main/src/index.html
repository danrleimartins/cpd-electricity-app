Electricity Bill

What Am I?

This is a desktop application for electricity users. By using this application, the user can calculate their electriciy bill using a unit rate (flat rate of 20 cents per unit), add standing charges at a rate of 4 cents per day and apply the VAT tariff at a rate of 13.5% to the total amount.
